Company

Who we are

Founded in 1999 by Joaquín Serna and Francisco Giner. What started with two hard-working friends has today grown into a company of great prestige.
Through time we have acquired new knowledge, partners and values that strengthen our work.

We are a company committed to achieve the highest standards of excellence. And that's why we are clear that in order to obtain a final product of the highest quality, it's essential to have materials and personnel according to that quality.

Our mission

We want to build works that will last for generations and generate a positive contribution to their residents and their environment. That's the reason why we emphasize so much on the importance of quality and commitment to our clients.

Our vision

Our goal is to be a world reference in Latin American construction, which's why we are in a constant process of improvement, continually increasing our personnel, incorporating new partners and acquiring new technologies.

Our values

  • Communication: We maintain constant communication, both internally and externally, accompanied by great mutual respect.
  • Integrity: Our work is totally transparent, there are no funds or decisions that aren't consulted.
  • Efficiency: We value productivity, the efficient use of materials, time and labor.
  • Commitment: We are committed to meeting each deadline and achieve the agreed quality and budget.
